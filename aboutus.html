HOW WERE WE CREATED?

The Sun Gym Gang operated in Miami, Florida, in the mid-1990s. They are responsible for the murders of Frank Grigi and Christina Firton, as well as for the kidnapping and extortion of Mark Schiller. The gang consisted mainly of bodybuilders who frequented the Sun Gym in Miami Lakes, Florida, including Daniel Lugo and Adrian Dorbal. Pete Collins published a three-part series in the Miami New Times in 1999 and 2000, which tells the story of a gang called "Pain and Profit", which was then adapted into the 2013 film Pain and Profit.
